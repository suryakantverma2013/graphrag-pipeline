# Gizmo G-3 Field Notes

The Gizmo G-3 is a handheld calibration tool for flow sensors. These notes
cover battery replacement and the zero-point calibration routine.

## Battery

Open the rear cover and replace the two AA cells, observing polarity. The unit
retains its calibration constants in non-volatile memory across a battery swap.

## Zero Calibration

With no flow applied, press and hold the ZERO key for three seconds until the
display reads 0.0. Repeat the procedure if the reading drifts by more than one
percent over a minute.
